Dorothy has returned to Oz. She has learned that it has been taken over by the Gnome King. He has turned everyone to stone and captured her friends, Scarecrow, Jack and Tik-Tok. She has traveled to the the Gnome King's Mountain to rescue them. He tells Dorothy that he has her friends here in his palace and he will give her a chance to save them. But he has turned them into onrmanets.

She has to guess which ornaments they have been turned into. If she picks the correct ones she will get to leave with 1, 2, or all 3 of her friends. However she can only guess incorrectly 4 times. Whoever is left behind will be lost forever.
